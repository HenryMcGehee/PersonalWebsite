About

I am a full stack software engineer and web designer focused largely on the objective of creating apps and utilities that assist in user productivity. I first developed interest in code languages while working as a sound designer for games. I soon realized that programming work would be a more suitable career path with the changing social and economic landscape, as adaptability is one of my core values as a creator and designer. Through completing the User Experience Design program with General Assembly as well as working on my own personal projects, I have developed a wide range of programming skills across many languages and applications. Regardless of the medium, however, I am always intensely motivated by the idea of bringing a concept to realization.
